Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana, popularly known as S-VYASA University, is a UGC-approved Deemed-to-be University. The university has taken a comprehensive approach to offering quality education and training while focusing on advancing research across a wide range of disciplines.

S-VYASA Deemed-to-be University, School of Advanced Studies, strives to impart core values that can aid in shaping individuals to become the future of tomorrow. Below is a list of core values that align with the S-VYASA University objectives.
